Adidas Brochure

A mock brochure for Adida's line of Ji Won Choi collection

In this project, my task was to create a brochure for an event or place. My choice for this project was Ji Won Choi's custom line of sports wear for Adidas. Ji Won Choi is a fashion designer, whose known for her bold lines and shapes in her work. I recorded the whole project for a different class, and made a short video explaining my process of creating this brochure.
